Tim Ferriss

Timothy "Tim" Ferriss is an American author, entrepreneur, angel investor, and public speaker.

Timeline

  • 1977: Born on July 20th in East Hampton, NY.
  • 2000: Received a degree in East Asian Studies from Princeton University.
  • 2001: Founded BrainQUICKEN, an online nutritional supplements company.
  • 2007: The Four Hour Workweek is published by Random House.
  • 2008: Had a pilot on the History Channel called Trial by Fire.
  • 2013: Premiered a TV show called the Tim Ferriss Experiment on HLN.
